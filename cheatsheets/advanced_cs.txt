### TECHNIQUES
- Algebraic manipulation: Try to apply thepremise to target terms.
- Counterexamples: Test with small finite magmas (size 2-4).
- Linear Models: Test if x*y = ax + by + c holds for some coefficients.
- Symmetries: Remember left-right duality.
- Invariants: Look for properties preserved by the operation.

### KEY RULES
- Magmas are sets with a single binary operation.
